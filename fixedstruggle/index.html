What is Fixed Struggle?

Fixed Struggle represents a situation where you experience constant struggle. Its an almost constant struggle living in poverty and trying to improve your situation. Currently, 1 billion children worldwide are living in poverty. The odds that a child who is born into poverty of having the same future status is high. Education is the best hope we have to break the cycle of multi-generational poverty. Fixed Struggle was created to spread awareness and create a conversation about giving each kid the opportunity and the resources to achieve greatness.

The Cause

I've started an education fundraiser with Mayan Families. I was introduced to their cause through the Living on One Dollar documentary. Their multi-dimensional approach to fighting poverty in Guatamala has helped improve the lives for thousands of Guatemalan families.

The Challenge

Bike as many hills and peaks on my fixed gear bicycle as possible to see how many feet of elevation gain I can climb.

My first goal is to reach the height of Mt. Fuji. Standing at 12,389 ft, Mt. Fuji is the tallest mountain in Japan. Due to the sheer quantity of elevation in Colorado, twelve-thousand feet should be achievable within my first handful of rides. If I make it to the top of Mt. Fuji, I'll set a new milestone and so on.

The Struggle

I'll be riding my fixed gear Fuji bicycle, this means I wont have any ease of gears when I take on elevation grade. Similiar to people in poverty have no "easy" button to bring relief when times get hard.
