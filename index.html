What is Fixed Struggle?

Fixed Struggle is very much a play on how living in poverty is a situation of an almost constant struggle. Currently, 1 billion children worldwide are living in poverty. The odds that a child who is born into poverty of having the same future status is high. Education is the best hope we have to break the cycle of multi-generational poverty. Fixed Struggle was created to spread awareness and create a conversation about giving each kid the opportunity and the resources to achieve greatness.
